Our Mission

Chess Explorers exists to bring the lifelong benefits of chess to children in daycares and less fortunate communities, with no cost barrier.

Why Chess?

Chess is more than a game. It teaches planning, patience, creativity, and resilience. For many kids—especially those in under-resourced neighborhoods or daycares—structured enrichment activities like chess are hard to access.

By offering completely free lessons where kids already are, such as daycares and youth programs, we remove barriers and create welcoming spaces where every child can explore, learn, and grow.

What We Believe

Access for All

Every child deserves access to enriching activities, regardless of income, background, or location. We bring chess directly to them.

Student Leadership

Our programs are led by dedicated student volunteers who design lessons, mentor younger players, and model leadership and kindness.

Community Impact

We partner with daycares, youth programs, and community spaces to provide a positive, structured activity that supports families and staff.

Our Goals

  • Connect with daycares, youth programs, and community centers that serve less fortunate kids.
  • Run regular, well-organized chess sessions and workshops at no cost.
  • Provide age-appropriate curricula from complete beginner to more advanced players.
  • Offer mentorship and leadership opportunities for student volunteers.
