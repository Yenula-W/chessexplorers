Bringing Chess to Daycares & Less Fortunate Kids, Completely Free.

Chess Explorers is a student-led organization that brings free, fun chess lessons to children in daycares and under-resourced communities. We build confidence, creativity, and critical thinking one move at a time.

Who We Serve

Kids & Families

Fun, welcoming chess sessions where every child is invited—no experience needed. We especially support kids from less fortunate backgrounds.

Daycares & Youth Programs

We visit daycares, after-care programs, and community youth spaces to run structured, engaging chess sessions at no cost.

Local Communities

Chess is a tool for connection and growth. We bring workshops and events to neighborhood spaces, shelters, and nonprofits.

How Chess Explorers Works

1

We Connect With Daycares & Programs

We coordinate with daycares, youth centers, and community organizations that would like free chess sessions for their kids.

2

We Bring Lessons & Materials

Our volunteers arrive with structured lessons, activities, and puzzles tailored to age and experience level.

3

We Grow Confident Young Thinkers

Kids build problem-solving skills, resilience, and creativity that go far beyond the chessboard.
